Development timeline and strategic implementation roadmap for yacht sharing platform

PHASE OVERVIEW

The Product Phase represents the critical transition from strategic planning to tactical implementation. This 6-week intensive development cycle focuses on comprehensive user experience mapping, brand integration, technical architecture planning, and mobile-first design implementation to ensure seamless handoffs between research, design, and development teams while maintaining alignment with our strategic objectives outlined in previous phases.
