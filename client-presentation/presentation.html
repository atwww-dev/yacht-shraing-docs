What Frustrates Users Today?

Current platforms have common problems that leave both renters and owners unhappy.

❌ Common Complaints

  • Hidden fees & surprise charges
  • Poor earnings for boat owners (15-35% fees)
  • Last-minute cancellations with no help
  • Slow or non-existent customer support

✅ Our Solutions

  • Live fee calculators for 100% transparency
  • Lower booking fees for better owner earnings
  • Clear, fair cancellation rules & real support
  • A platform that respects both sides of the rental

Challenges to Watch For

Building a trusted platform means finding the right balance.

Trust vs. Convenience

Thorough verification is key for safety, but it can slow down sign-ups. We must make this process as smooth and fast as possible.

High User Expectations

Users expect a fast, premium, and bug-free experience. Our platform needs to be polished and reliable from day one.
