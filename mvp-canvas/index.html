VISION

Unlock idle yachts for profit and let skilled sailors book with confidence—on a mobile-first, transparent P2P marketplace
Owner promise: Earn more from your boat—hassle-free, fully in control.
Renter promise: Premium, verified yachts—no surprises, just smooth sailing.

USER PERSONAS

Boat Owner

Who: Tech-savvy hobbyist, not a tech expert

  • Needs: Simple listing, clear earnings, safety controls, flexible & easy transparency
  • Goals: Maximize ROI, improve boat booking upon registration

Professional Skipper

Who: Experienced sailor who wants to get a good quality boat

  • Needs: Verified boats, transparent fees, direct owner contact, quality guarantees
  • Success: trust factor upon quality, quick bookings, easy access

VALUE PROPOSITION

One app that delivers:

  1. Transparent Earnings: See your net take immediately, with no hidden fees - boat owners will have full transparency on the money they make.
  2. Lower Fees: We take 10% instead of the other solutions where a high fees starting from around 15% - WE DO LESS!
  3. Dual-Layer Trust: KYC + Biometrics for everyone, OCR-verified boat docs. Everyone needs to verify their identities to ensure trust on the platform.
  4. Cross Review System - everyone gets a review
  5. Flexible Booking: Instant book or chat first requests.
  6. Smart Engagement: Push reminders for uploads, photos, reviews.

USPS

  • Live Earnings Calculator: Owners know exactly what they make, upfront and transparently. When the owner list their boat they will automatically see estimated incomes based on data + transparent API per hour.
  • Skipper Skill Match: List your boat in minutes. App documents, auto-populate categories. We create a system which leverages AI and OCR tech to make onboarding & listing boats a sweet dream.
  • Two-Sided Safety Net: Owners feel 100% safe and protected. No one gets stolen!
